§\S§8.4 复合函数求导

一、多元函数的链式求导法则

1. 中间变量为一元函数

定理：
若 u=ϕ(t)u=\phi(t)u=ϕ(t)、v=ψ(t)v=\psi(t)v=ψ(t) 在 ttt 处可导，且 z=f(u,v)z=f(u,v)z=f(u,v) 在 (u,v)(u,v)(u,v) 处偏导连续，则 z=f[ϕ(t),ψ(t)]z=f[\phi(t),\psi(t)]z=f[ϕ(t),ψ(t)] 在 ttt 可导，且
dzdt=∂f∂u⋅dudt+∂f∂v⋅dvdt\begin{align*} \dfrac{\mathrm dz}{\mathrm dt}=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial u}\cdot \dfrac{\mathrm du}{\mathrm dt}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial v}\cdot \dfrac{\mathrm dv}{\mathrm dt} \end{align*}dtdz​=∂u∂f​⋅dtdu​+∂v∂f​⋅dtdv​​

z=f(u,v,w)z=f(u,v,w)z=f(u,v,w)，u=u(t)u=u(t)u=u(t)，v=v(t)v=v(t)v=v(t)，w=w(t)w=w(t)w=w(t) 在 ttt 处可导，偏导连续，有
dzdt=∂f∂u⋅dudt+∂f∂v⋅dvdt+∂f∂w⋅dwdt\begin{align*} \dfrac{\mathrm dz}{\mathrm dt}=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial u}\cdot \dfrac{\mathrm du}{\mathrm dt}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial v}\cdot \dfrac{\mathrm dv}{\mathrm dt}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial w}\cdot \dfrac{\mathrm dw}{\mathrm dt} \end{align*}dtdz​=∂u∂f​⋅dtdu​+∂v∂f​⋅dtdv​+∂w∂f​⋅dtdw​​

2. 中间变量为多元函数

若 z=f(u,v)z=f(u,v)z=f(u,v) 在 (u,v)(u,v)(u,v) 处偏导连续，且 u=ϕ(x,y)u=\phi(x,y)u=ϕ(x,y)、v=ψ(x,y)v=\psi(x,y)v=ψ(x,y) 在 (x,y)(x,y)(x,y) 偏导存在，则 z=f[ϕ(x,y),ψ(x,y)]z=f[\phi(x,y),\psi(x,y)]z=f[ϕ(x,y),ψ(x,y)] 在 (x,y)(x,y)(x,y) 偏导存在，且
∂z∂x=∂f∂u⋅∂u∂x+∂f∂v⋅∂v∂x∂z∂y=∂f∂u⋅∂u∂y+∂f∂v⋅∂v∂y\begin{align*} \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial x}=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial u}\cdot \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial v}\cdot \dfrac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial y}=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial u}\cdot \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial v}\cdot \dfrac{\partial v}{\partial y} \end{align*}∂x∂z​=∂u∂f​⋅∂x∂u​+∂v∂f​⋅∂x∂v​∂y∂z​=∂u∂f​⋅∂y∂u​+∂v∂f​⋅∂y∂v​​

w=f(u)w=f(u)w=f(u)，u=u(x,y,z)u=u(x,y,z)u=u(x,y,z)，有
∂w∂x=dfdu⋅∂u∂x∂w∂y=dfdu⋅∂u∂y∂w∂z=dfdu⋅∂u∂z\begin{align*} \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x}=\dfrac{\mathrm df}{\mathrm du}\cdot \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial x}\\ \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y}=\dfrac{\mathrm df}{\mathrm du}\cdot \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial y}\\ \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z}=\dfrac{\mathrm df}{\mathrm du}\cdot \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial z} \end{align*}∂x∂w​=dudf​⋅∂x∂u​∂y∂w​=dudf​⋅∂y∂u​∂z∂w​=dudf​⋅∂z∂u​​

3. 中间变量既有一元函数，又有二元函数

z=f[ϕ(x,y),ψ(y)]z=f[\phi(x,y),\psi(y)]z=f[ϕ(x,y),ψ(y)] 在 (x,y)(x,y)(x,y) 偏导存在，有
∂z∂x=∂f∂u⋅∂u∂x∂z∂y=∂f∂u⋅∂u∂y+∂f∂v⋅dvdy\begin{align*} \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial u}\cdot \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial x}\\ \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial u}\cdot \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial v}\cdot \dfrac{\mathrm dv}{\mathrm dy} \end{align*}∂x∂z​∂y∂z​​=∂u∂f​⋅∂x∂u​=∂u∂f​⋅∂y∂u​+∂v∂f​⋅dydv​​

4. 中间变量也是自变量

若 u=ϕ(x,y)u=\phi(x,y)u=ϕ(x,y) 在 (x,y)(x,y)(x,y) 偏导存在，且 z=f(u,x,y)z=f(u,x,y)z=f(u,x,y) 在 (u,x,y)(u,x,y)(u,x,y) 偏导连续，则 z=f[ϕ(x,y),x,y]z=f[\phi(x,y),x,y]z=f[ϕ(x,y),x,y] 在 (x,y)(x,y)(x,y) 偏导存在
∂z∂x=∂f∂u⋅∂y∂x+∂f∂x⋅dxdx∂z∂y=∂f∂u⋅∂u∂y+∂f∂y⋅1\begin{align*} \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial u}\cdot \dfrac{\partial y}{\partial x}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}\cdot \dfrac{\mathrm dx}{\mathrm dx}\\ \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial u}\cdot \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}\cdot 1 \end{align*}∂x∂z​∂y∂z​​=∂u∂f​⋅∂x∂y​+∂x∂f​⋅dxdx​=∂u∂f​⋅∂y∂u​+∂y∂f​⋅1​

二、一阶全微分的形式不变性

z=f(x,y)z=f(x,y)z=f(x,y)（x,yx,yx,y 为自变量），dz=∂f∂xdx+∂f∂ydy\mathrm dz=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}\mathrm dx+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}\mathrm dydz=∂x∂f​dx+∂y∂f​dy

x=ϕ(x,t)x=\phi(x,t)x=ϕ(x,t)，y=ψ(s,t)y=\psi(s,t)y=ψ(s,t)（x,yx,yx,y 为中间变量），
dz=∂z∂sds+∂z∂tdt=(∂f∂x⋅∂ϕ∂s+∂f∂y⋅∂ψ∂s)ds（或(∂f∂x⋅∂ϕ∂t+∂f∂y⋅∂ψ∂s)dt）=∂f∂x(∂ϕ∂sds+∂ψ∂tdt)dx+∂f∂y(∂ϕ∂sds+∂ψ∂tdt)dy\begin{align*} \mathrm dz&=\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial s}\mathrm ds+\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial t}\mathrm dt\\ &=(\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}\cdot \dfrac{\partial \phi}{\partial s}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}\cdot \dfrac{\partial \psi}{\partial s})\mathrm ds（或 (\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}\cdot \dfrac{\partial \phi}{\partial t}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}\cdot \dfrac{\partial \psi}{\partial s})\mathrm dt）\\ &=\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}\underset{\mathrm dx}{(\dfrac{\partial \phi}{\partial s}\mathrm ds+\dfrac{\partial \psi}{\partial t}\mathrm dt)}+\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}\underset{\mathrm dy}{(\dfrac{\partial \phi}{\partial s}\mathrm ds+\dfrac{\partial \psi}{\partial t}\mathrm dt)} \end{align*}dz​=∂s∂z​ds+∂t∂z​dt=(∂x∂f​⋅∂s∂ϕ​+∂y∂f​⋅∂s∂ψ​)ds（或(∂x∂f​⋅∂t∂ϕ​+∂y∂f​⋅∂s∂ψ​)dt）=∂x∂f​dx(∂s∂ϕ​ds+∂t∂ψ​dt)​+∂y∂f​dy(∂s∂ϕ​ds+∂t∂ψ​dt)​​
